DESIGN PROJECT

DESIGN PROJECT

​Interior design is a visionary art of aesthetics and functionality. Visualizing interior in 3D software, is a best option to showcase the best of design and creative thinking. Client will receive interior design project album, which will outline all the details and drawings, also specifications for color scheme, lighting, furniture and accessories including price, samples, and trade discount where applicable.
STYLING AND DECORATING

STYLING AND DECORATING

STYLING AND DECORATING A mood board is a collection of ideas and selections, that designer is proposing to a client. It includes furniture, lights, decor and other selections like material and finishes presented together. This service is ideal if you are a landlord, developer, or private owner preparing a property for sale or rent
DESIGN SERVICE ON A DISTANCE

DESIGN SERVICE ON A DISTANCE

For a clients who are willing to work on a distance, are offered same services of interior design and decorating. The only difference is a way of communicating with a client - in person meetings are replaced with online communication. Client will receive interior design or interior decorating project album.
